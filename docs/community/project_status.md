# Project Status

## Current Stage

The Human Capability Project is in its formative stage.

The project's purpose, mission, and initial research topics have been established, but the broader structure of the community is intentionally being developed through collaboration rather than predetermined by a single author.

---

## Current Focus

The community is currently organizing around three research topics:

- AI Governance
- Continuity
- Transparency

Additional research topics may emerge as contributors identify new areas of shared interest.

---

## What Is Established

- Project mission
- Foundational principles
- Initial project charter
- Repository structure
- Initial research topics

---

## What Is Intentionally Open

The following areas are expected to evolve through community discussion and contribution:

- Community governance
- Contribution workflows
- Research organization
- Topic expansion
- Collaboration practices
- Long-term organizational structure

---

## Guiding Philosophy

THCP is intended to grow through transparent collaboration.

Rather than attempting to define every process in advance, we aim to establish a strong foundation and allow the community to help shape how the project evolves over time.
