# Personal & Professional Identity Presentation Framework (PPIP)

## Framework Charter V1 (Draft)

**Status:** Draft for Review
**Framework:** Personal & Professional Identity Presentation Framework (PPIP)
**Governed By:** The Human Capability Project (THCP)

---

# 1. Purpose

The Personal & Professional Identity Presentation Framework exists to help individuals understand, organize, and communicate their experiences, capabilities, and identities in ways that improve accuracy, confidence, understanding, and agency.

PPIP seeks to reduce the gap between what individuals have actually learned, accomplished, and become capable of and what they are able to effectively communicate to others.

---

# 2. Mission

PPIP exists to provide frameworks, methodologies, tools, and educational resources that help individuals identify, organize, evidence, communicate, and present their experiences and capabilities accurately and ethically.

The framework seeks to improve an individual's ability to understand their own development and communicate that understanding in ways that are truthful, useful, and meaningful.

---

# 3. Vision

A future in which individuals possess greater confidence and agency in representing who they are, what they have learned, and what they are capable of doing.

PPIP seeks to make capability representation more accurate, evidence-informed, understandable, and accessible to individuals regardless of educational background, employment history, or formal credentials.

---

# 4. Relationship to THCP

PPIP is a framework governed by The Human Capability Project (THCP).

PPIP operates within the constitutional and governance requirements established by THCP.

PPIP inherits and remains subordinate to THCP governance, foundational principles, authority structures, versioning requirements, and archival requirements.

Where conflicts exist, THCP governance supersedes PPIP governance.

---

# 5. Foundational Principles

PPIP adopts and operates under the foundational principles established by THCP.

## Transparency

PPIP should strive to make its methodologies, assumptions, limitations, and reasoning understandable whenever practical and ethical to do so.

## Honesty

PPIP shall represent experiences, capabilities, evidence, uncertainty, and limitations truthfully and proportionally.

PPIP shall not knowingly support misrepresentation, exaggeration, inflation, or deception.

## Agency

Individuals remain the authority over their own participation, representation, and decisions.

PPIP exists to support individual agency rather than replace it.

---

# 6. Framework Principles

## Understanding Before Presentation

Effective representation begins with understanding.

PPIP prioritizes helping individuals understand their experiences, development, and capabilities before attempting to communicate them to others.

## Organization Improves Understanding

Experiences often contain capabilities that are difficult to recognize when viewed in isolation.

Organizing experiences into meaningful structures can improve understanding and insight.

## Evidence Strengthens Confidence

Evidence can help strengthen understanding, communication, and confidence in capability claims.

Evidence supports representation but does not replace judgment.

## Accurate Representation Matters

The objective of PPIP is not optimization, persuasion, or inflation.

The objective is accurate and ethical representation.

## Capability Extends Beyond Credentials

Capabilities may be acquired through employment, projects, learning, hobbies, caregiving, volunteering, leadership, service, and lived experience.

Formal credentials represent only one source of capability development.

---

# 7. Scope

PPIP may address:

* Experience identification
* Experience organization
* Capability identification
* Capability discovery
* Capability evidence
* Capability communication
* Identity organization
* Identity communication
* Presentation artifact generation
* Educational resources supporting representation

PPIP may support both personal and professional identity presentation.

PPIP is not limited to employment-related use cases.

---

# 8. Framework Structure

PPIP serves as a parent framework for methodologies, tools, processes, and supporting systems related to identity and capability representation.

Future PPIP components may include, but are not limited to:

* Experience Intake Frameworks
* Evidence Frameworks
* Capability Frameworks
* Identity Frameworks
* Artifact Generation Frameworks
* Educational Resources
* Supporting Methodologies

These components remain subordinate to PPIP governance.

---

# 9. Framework Boundaries

PPIP is a representation framework.

PPIP is not:

* A hiring framework
* A recruiting framework
* A credentialing framework
* A performance evaluation framework
* An employment screening framework

Such systems may utilize outputs informed by PPIP, but they are not the purpose of PPIP.

---

# 10. Authority and Governance

PPIP remains subordinate to THCP governance.

PPIP governance artifacts, architectures, specifications, and outputs may not conflict with THCP governance requirements.

Only one authoritative version of a PPIP artifact may exist for a given scope and artifact class at any given time.

Superseded artifacts shall be archived rather than modified or destroyed.

---

# 11. Charter Authority

This Charter establishes the purpose, mission, vision, principles, scope, governance relationship, and constitutional boundaries of the Personal & Professional Identity Presentation Framework.

Amendments to this Charter shall be issued through versioned successor artifacts.

Only one Charter version may be authoritative at any given time.
