# The Human Capability Project (THCP)

## Project Charter V2

**Status:** Current Direction
**Project:** The Human Capability Project (THCP)

---

# 1. Purpose

The Human Capability Project is an open collaborative research community.

THCP exists to support shared inquiry, discussion, documentation, and collaboration around questions affecting human capability, agency, institutions, technology, and society.

---

# 2. Current Direction

THCP is not centered on any one framework, architecture, methodology, or personal project.

The project is intended to provide a place where contributors can explore related questions together while retaining ownership of their own work, ideas, projects, and contributions.

THCP may host discussion, notes, research documents, references, experiments, and collaborative artifacts, but participation in THCP does not transfer ownership of a contributor's independent work to the project.

---

# 3. Initial Research Topics

THCP is currently beginning with three initial areas of inquiry:

* AI Governance
* Continuity
* Transparency

These topics are starting points for community exploration, not fixed boundaries for the project.

---

# 4. Open Development

THCP is intentionally leaving future governance, topic expansion, and project structure open for contributor input.

The community may later decide how to organize research areas, shared documents, decision-making practices, and collaboration processes.

Until then, THCP should remain simple, open, and adaptable.

---

# 5. Historical Materials

Earlier documents in this repository may reflect prior drafts, frameworks, or personal project directions.

Those materials are preserved for historical context and traceability. They should not be treated as current THCP initiatives unless a future community decision explicitly adopts them.
