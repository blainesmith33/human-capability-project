# Getting Started

Welcome to **The Human Capability Project (THCP)**.

THCP is an open collaborative research community exploring how humans, institutions, and AI can work together to expand human capability while preserving transparency, honesty, agency, accountability, and human responsibility.

This repository exists to support collaborative inquiry, shared learning, and the development of ideas through open discussion and documentation.

---

# Before You Begin

You do **not** need to adopt someone else's architecture.

You do **not** need to abandon your own work.

You do **not** need to agree with existing ideas.

Many contributors approach similar problems from different perspectives, using different terminology, assumptions, and methodologies.

THCP exists to provide a place where those ideas can be explored together—without requiring them to become the same.

---

# Ways to Participate

There are many ways to contribute.

You might:

- Ask thoughtful questions.
- Share observations.
- Contribute research.
- Publish articles or references.
- Develop software or tools.
- Propose frameworks or methodologies.
- Improve documentation.
- Offer constructive criticism.
- Participate in discussions.

Every meaningful contribution helps improve our collective understanding.

---

# Current Research Topics

THCP is intentionally beginning with a small number of research topics:

- AI Governance
- Continuity
- Transparency

These are simply the starting points.

As the community grows, additional research topics may emerge through community discussion and collaboration.

---

# Community Principles

We encourage contributors to:

- Be honest about what they know and what they do not know.
- Explain assumptions where practical.
- Respect differing perspectives.
- Challenge ideas rather than individuals.
- Distinguish observations from conclusions.
- Welcome evidence that improves understanding.

The objective is not to build consensus around a single framework.

The objective is to better understand complex problems together.

---

# An Evolving Community

THCP is still in its early stages.

Many aspects of the project—including its organization, governance, research practices, and long-term direction—are intentionally being left open so they can be shaped through community participation.

If this approach resonates with you, we invite you to join the conversation and help build what comes next.
